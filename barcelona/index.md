## What Makes the Abroad Edition Different

<div class="card-grid card-grid--2">

<div class="feature-card">
<strong>CRAFT Pedagogy in Every Session</strong>
<p>Contextualize → Reframe → Assemble → Fortify → Transfer. Each 2.5-hour session follows this research-backed cycle.</p>
</div>

<div class="feature-card">
<strong>Industry Context on Location</strong>
<p>Academic visits replace textbook motivation — you see HDL concepts in production before you build them in lab.</p>
</div>

<div class="feature-card">
<strong>Same Rigor, Tighter Arc</strong>
<p>14 teaching sessions cover the full 16-day curriculum. Two strategic merges, zero cut corners.</p>
</div>

<div class="feature-card">
<strong>Open-Source Everything</strong>
<p>No license servers, no vendor lock-in — the same iCE40 toolchain travels with you.</p>
</div>
