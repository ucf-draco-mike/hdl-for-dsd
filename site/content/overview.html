A 4-week intensive course taking students from zero HDL experience to confidently designing, simulating, and implementing digital systems in Verilog on real FPGA hardware. Uses an entirely open-source toolchain (Yosys, nextpnr, Icarus Verilog) and integrates modern practices including AI-assisted verification and PPA analysis.
