Day 10 Lab: Numerical Architectures & Design Trade-offs

Overview

Today you'll compare adder and multiplier architectures, implement a sequential multiplier, work with fixed-point arithmetic, and produce your first structured PPA comparison table. These are the same analysis habits you'll use in the final project report.
